About Our Dropout Support Program

We understand that the path to academic success isn’t always straightforward. Life circumstances, mental health challenges, financial difficulties, or academic struggles can make it hard to stay on course. We want you to know that you’re not alone, and we’re here to support you every step of the way.

Our Dropout Support Program provides resources, guidance, and encouragement to help you overcome obstacles and, if possible, continue toward your educational goals.

What We Offer

  • Personalized Counseling: One-on-one sessions with academic and career advisors to help you navigate your challenges and plan for the future.
  • Academic Assistance: Access to tutoring and other learning resources to help with any academic difficulties.
  • Financial Aid Guidance: Support in understanding and applying for financial assistance, scholarships, and grants.
  • Mental Health Support: Access to resources and counseling focused on mental health and well-being with games related to it.
  • Re-Enrollment Assistance: Help with re-enrollment for students who have previously withdrawn or taken a break.

Our Commitment to You

Our goal is to provide every student with the tools they need to thrive academically, personally, and professionally. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and supportive environment where all students feel empowered to pursue their dreams, no matter the challenges.

If you or someone you know is considering dropping out, please reach out. Together, we can explore options and create a plan that works best for you.
